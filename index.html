How to Use the Seating Suite

1. Navigation & Camera

  • Zoom: Use your mouse scroll wheel, or the ➕/➖ buttons.
  • Pan (Move Camera): Hold Shift on your keyboard, then click and drag the background.

2. Building the Classroom

  • Use the right sidebar to spawn desks, rugs, and walls.
  • Resize/Rotate: Click any asset to reveal the blue control handles. Drag corners to resize, drag the top handle to rotate.
  • Lock Desks: Click the "Desks: UNLOCKED" button in the bottom left to freeze all desks so you don't accidentally move them while assigning students.
  • Lock / Rename Assets: Double-click any non-desk asset (like a rug or window) to rename it and lock it into place. Double-click it again to unlock it.

3. Assigning Students

  • Manual Drag & Drop: Select a class period on the left. Drag a student's name from the list and drop them directly onto an empty desk.
  • Auto-Assign: Click the "🎲 Auto-Assign" button to let the algorithm place all remaining students. It will prioritize placing "FRONT" preferred students first, and will attempt to keep restricted pairs far apart.
  • Lock a Student: Double-click a student's desk to lock them into that seat. The Auto-Assigner will build the rest of the room around them.
  • Block an Empty Desk: Double-click an empty desk to mark it as "Blocked". The Auto-Assigner will leave it empty.
  • Remove a Student: Hold Alt and click a student's desk to instantly clear that seat.
